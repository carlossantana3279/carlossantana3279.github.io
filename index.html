About

Hi, I'm Carlos, a hard working student completing my final year of a Bachelors Computer Science Program at McMaster University. I am currently seeking a full-time position in my field in Ontario after graduation (April 2019). Aside from my education at McMaster University, I have successfully completed a 16 month internship at Bell Canada in Mississauga Ontario as a software developer. As an entrepreneur, I started a software company that creates websites for small businesses and creates mobile applications. An app that I developed was Touch Roulette – Decision Maker, a cross platform app to help groups make decisions quickly in a fun way has over 45,000 downloads and a 3.8/5 rating on the Play Store.

At Bell I was responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining in-house tools used by the Core Network Team. Much of my work focused on creating applications that centered around helping the Core Network Team improve data driven decision making by visualizing data and automation of the Network. I have extensive experience using .Net Core since it was the main framework used to create new web applications. I also thrived in my role as the Scrum Master and facilitated bi-weekly workshops for 6 months for a team of 5 members. Some of my hobbies include photography, developing mobile apps, actively contributing to Google Maps(being a Local Guide), playing team sports, and traveling. To learn more about me, keep reading!
